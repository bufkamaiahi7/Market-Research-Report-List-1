<p><p>The plasma treater market is segmented into two main types: ground type plasma treaters and portable plasma treaters. Ground type plasma treaters are typically larger systems installed in manufacturing facilities, designed for high-throughput and continuous processing of materials like plastics and metals. In contrast, portable plasma treaters offer flexibility and are designed for smaller-scale applications, enabling users to treat surfaces on-site or in varying locations, making them ideal for smaller businesses or specialized tasks. Both types improve surface adhesion, cleanliness, and coating effectiveness.</p></p>
