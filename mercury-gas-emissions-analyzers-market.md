<p><strong>The Mercury Gas Emissions Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption</li><li>Cold Atomic Fluorescence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mercury Gas Emissions Analyzers market encompasses technologies like Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption (CVAA) and Cold Atomic Fluorescence (CAF). CVAA utilizes a mercury vapor's absorption characteristics to quantify emissions, offering high sensitivity and selectivity. In contrast, CAF leverages the fluorescent light emitted by mercury atoms under excitation, enabling accurate detection at low concentrations. Both methods excel in monitoring environmental mercury levels, playing a crucial role in regulatory compliance and pollution control strategies across various industries.</p></p>
