<p><p>The smartphone screen protector market is primarily segmented into three types: Tempered Glass, Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET), and Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU). Tempered Glass protectors are durable and offer high transparency, providing excellent scratch resistance and impact protection. PET protectors are thin and lightweight, offering basic protection against scratches but less shock resistance. TPU protectors are flexible and self-healing, providing a good balance of durability and clarity while effectively absorbing shocks. Each type serves different user preferences and protection needs.</p></p>
