<p><p>The 5G Microcells market can be categorized into two main types based on frequency bands: FR1 (Sub-6 GHz) and FR2 (mmWave). FR1 encompasses lower frequency bands, providing wider coverage and enhanced penetration, making it suitable for urban and rural deployments. In contrast, FR2 operates at higher frequencies, offering ultra-fast data rates and lower latency, ideal for densely populated areas. Together, these microcells enable enhanced connectivity, supporting various applications like IoT, mobile broadband, and smart city initiatives.</p></p>
