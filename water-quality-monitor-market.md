<p><p>The water quality monitor market is categorized into single parameter and multi-parameter monitors. In contrast, multi-parameter monitors simultaneously measure multiple parameters, providing a comprehensive overview of water quality in various applications. The choice between them depends on the monitoring needs, budget, and complexity of the water quality assessment required, catering to diverse industries and environmental concerns.</p></p>
