<p><strong>The Synchronous Electric Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DC Excited Synchronous Electric Motors</li><li>Non-Excited Synchronous Electric Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synchronous electric motors market is primarily divided into two types: DC excited synchronous motors and non-excited synchronous motors. DC excited synchronous motors utilize a direct current supply to energize the rotor's winding, providing reliable performance and precise speed control, often used in industrial applications. Non-excited synchronous motors, on the other hand, feature permanent magnets on the rotor, eliminating the need for external excitation. This design enhances efficiency and reduces maintenance, making them suitable for various applications, including renewable energy systems and electric vehicles.</p></p>
