<p><strong>The Dredger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cutter-Suction Dredger</li><li>Trailing Suction Hopper Dredger</li><li>Chain Bucket Dredge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dredger market consists of specialized equipment used for excavation and material removal from water bodies. Cutter-suction dredgers utilize a rotating cutter head to loosen sediment, which is then suctioned into a pump for transport. Trailing suction hopper dredgers feature an elongated suction pipe that trails behind, allowing for efficient sediment collection and storage in onboard hoppers. Chain bucket dredges consist of a series of buckets attached to a chain, which scoop up material from the seabed, making them suitable for deeper applications.</p></p>
