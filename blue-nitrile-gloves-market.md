<p><p>The blue nitrile gloves market is categorized into two main types: powder and powder-free. Powdered gloves are coated with cornstarch to facilitate easier donning, making them suitable for quick tasks and those who prefer a smoother application. However, they may pose a risk of respiratory allergies. Powder-free gloves, on the other hand, are designed without this coating, reducing allergy risks and providing a more tactile experience. This makes them ideal for medical applications and sensitive tasks requiring precision.</p></p>
