<p><p>Gluten-free bakery products are specially formulated items that cater to individuals with celiac disease or gluten intolerance, offering alternatives to traditional baked goods made with wheat, barley, or rye. These products include bread, cakes, cookies, pastries, and muffins, typically made from gluten-free flours such as rice, almond, or coconut flour. The demand for gluten-free items has surged in recent years, driven by a growing awareness of gluten-related disorders and the broader trend towards healthier eating habits.</p><p>The gluten-free bakery product market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.9% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to increasing consumer interest in gluten-free diets, not only for health reasons but also as a lifestyle choice. Trends influencing this sector include the introduction of innovative flavors and textures in gluten-free offerings, as well as the rise of ready-to-eat and convenient products. Additionally, the incorporation of organic and non-GMO ingredients is gaining traction, appealing to health-conscious consumers. The emergence of online retail channels has further facilitated market expansion by allowing easy access to a wider range of gluten-free products. Overall, the gluten-free bakery product market is poised for sustained development driven by evolving consumer preferences.</p></p>
