<p><p>The commercial griddle market includes two primary types: gas and electric griddles. Commercial gas griddles utilize natural gas or propane, offering rapid heating and high temperature control, making them ideal for high-volume cooking in restaurants. Each type caters to different kitchen needs, operational costs, and cooking preferences in the foodservice industry.</p></p>
