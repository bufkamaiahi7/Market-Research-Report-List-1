<p><strong>The Digital PCR Platform Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microfluidic Chip Digital PCR</li><li>Droplet Digital PCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital PCR platform market is primarily divided into two types: Microfluidic Chip Digital PCR and Droplet Digital PCR. Microfluidic Chip Digital PCR utilizes miniaturized fluidic networks to conduct precise reactions in tiny volumes, enabling high sensitivity and reduced reagent usage. In contrast, Droplet Digital PCR partitions the sample into thousands of droplets, allowing for individual reactions, enhancing quantification accuracy. Both technologies are essential for applications in oncology, infectious diseases, and genetic research, driving advancements in precision diagnostics.</p></p>
