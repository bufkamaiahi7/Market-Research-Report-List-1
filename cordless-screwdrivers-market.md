<p><p>The cordless screwdrivers market is segmented primarily into electrical and pneumatic types. Electrical cordless screwdrivers use battery power, offering convenience and portability for various tasks, from home improvement to professional construction. They are typically lightweight and user-friendly. In contrast, pneumatic cordless screwdrivers operate using compressed air, providing higher torque and faster speeds, ideal for heavy-duty applications in industrial settings.
