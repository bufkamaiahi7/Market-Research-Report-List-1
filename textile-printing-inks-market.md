<p><strong>The Textile Printing Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactive Dye Inks</li><li>Acidic Ink</li><li>Paint Ink</li><li>Dispersion & Sublimation Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The textile printing inks market encompasses various types tailored for different fabrics and applications. Reactive dye inks are ideal for cellulosic fibers, chemically bonding with substrates for vibrant colors. Acidic inks target protein fibers like silk and wool, offering vivid hues. Paint inks provide versatility for a range of materials, while dispersion inks are used for printing on synthetic substrates, ensuring stability and vibrancy. Sublimation inks are suited for polyester fabrics, enabling heat transfer techniques that produce durable, high-quality prints.</p></p>
