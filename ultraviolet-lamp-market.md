<p><ul><li>UV Mercury Lamp</li><li>UV LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultraviolet lamp market comprises two primary types: UV mercury lamps and UV LED lamps. UV mercury lamps utilize mercury vapor to produce ultraviolet light, commonly employed in applications such as water purification and industrial disinfection due to their high intensity and broad spectrum. In contrast, UV LED lamps offer energy efficiency and longer lifespans, making them ideal for portable and low-power applications, such as surface sterilization and curing processes. Both types serve critical roles in enhancing sanitation and efficiency across various industries.</p></p>
