<p><p>Lead smelting and refining involves several market types for extracting and purifying lead from ore or recycled sources. Pyrometallurgical processes involve high-temperature melting and chemical reactions to separate lead from impurities. Solvent extraction uses chemical solvents to selectively extract lead from solutions, enhancing purity. Ion exchange involves using resins to selectively capture lead ions from liquid solutions, offering an efficient purification method. These techniques cater to different operational needs and economic considerations within the lead market.</p></p>
