<p><p>The Extended Release Drug market includes Sustained Release and Controlled Release drugs, both designed to optimize drug delivery over time. Sustained Release drugs release active ingredients consistently at a uniform rate, maintaining therapeutic levels without significant peaks or troughs. Controlled Release drugs, on the other hand, offer a more sophisticated mechanism, allowing for predetermined release rates in response to physiological conditions. Together, they enhance patient compliance, reduce dosing frequency, and improve therapeutic outcomes by providing a steady supply of medication.</p></p>
