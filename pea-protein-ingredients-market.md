<p><p>The pea protein ingredients market is primarily categorized into three segments: isolates, concentrates, and other forms. Isolates are highly purified forms that contain around 85-90% protein, making them ideal for high-protein formulations. Concentrates, containing about 70-80% protein, retain more fiber and nutrients, offering a balanced option for food products. The "other" category encompasses various forms, such as textured pea protein and blends, used for specific applications like meat alternatives and protein bars, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
