<p><p>The soda lime glass market is broadly divided into two types: high soda lime glass and medium soda lime glass. High soda lime glass features a higher sodium content, enhancing its clarity and chemical durability, making it ideal for applications like premium tableware and laboratory instruments. Medium soda lime glass, on the other hand, contains a balanced composition that offers good thermal resistance and is commonly used in everyday products like bottles, jars, and window panes. Each type caters to specific industry needs and applications.</p></p>
