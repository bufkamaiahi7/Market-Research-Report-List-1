<p><p>The clip-on microphones market features various types tailored for specific recording situations. Omnidirectional microphones capture sound from all directions, making them ideal for interviews and group discussions. Cardioid microphones focus on sound from the front, minimizing background noise, suitable for solo performances and podcasts. Supercardioid microphones provide a narrower pickup pattern, offering even greater isolation of the sound source, often used in live events. Other types may include lavalier and boundary microphones, catering to diverse audio capture needs.</p></p>
