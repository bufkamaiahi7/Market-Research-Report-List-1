<p><p>The Permanent Magnet Variable Frequency Screw Vacuum Pump market is divided into two main types: Single Helix and Double Helix. Single Helix pumps feature a single helical rotor that compresses gas, offering simplicity and compact design, suitable for lower vacuum applications. In contrast, Double Helix pumps utilize two intermeshing helical rotors, providing enhanced efficiency, higher throughput, and superior vacuum levels, making them ideal for demanding industrial processes. Both types leverage variable frequency technology for optimized performance and energy efficiency.</p></p>
