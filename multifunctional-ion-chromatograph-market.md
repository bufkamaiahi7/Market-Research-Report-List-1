<p><strong>The Multifunctional Ion Chromatograph Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Ion Exchange Chromatography"</li><li>"Ion Exclusion Chromatography"</li><li>"Ion Pair Chromatography"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multifunctional ion chromatograph market encompasses three key types: Ion Exchange Chromatography, Ion Exclusion Chromatography, and Ion Pair Chromatography. Ion Exchange Chromatography specializes in separating ions based on their charge, making it ideal for analyzing charged analytes. Ion Exclusion Chromatography focuses on separating anions and neutral species, effectively excluding larger ions from the column. Ion Pair Chromatography utilizes a pairing agent to enhance the separation of ionic compounds in nonpolar solvents, combining features of both ion exchange and reversed-phase chromatography.</p></p>
