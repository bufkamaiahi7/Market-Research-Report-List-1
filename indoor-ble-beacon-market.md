<p><p>The indoor BLE beacon market comprises three main types: iBeacon, Eddystone, and AltBeacon. iBeacon, developed by Apple, focuses on proximity marketing and is compatible with iOS devices. Eddystone, created by Google, supports both Android and iOS and can transmit various payloads, including URLs and telemetry data. AltBeacon is an open-source alternative, allowing flexibility in implementation and compatibility with different platforms. Each type aims to enhance location-based services, enabling businesses to engage customers through targeted notifications and insights.</p></p>
