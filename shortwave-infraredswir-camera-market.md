<p><p>The Shortwave Infrared (SWIR) camera market consists primarily of two types: SWIR area cameras and SWIR linear cameras. SWIR area cameras capture images in a two-dimensional array, making them suitable for applications requiring detailed imaging, such as surveillance and industrial inspection. In contrast, SWIR linear cameras use a one-dimensional array to scan scenes linearly, which is ideal for high-speed applications like sorting or monitoring moving objects. Together, these technologies enhance visibility in low-light conditions and improve material analysis capabilities.</p></p>
