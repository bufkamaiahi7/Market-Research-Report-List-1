<p><ul><li>Fixed Pitch Propeller</li><li>Varying Pitch Propeller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airplane propeller market is primarily divided into two types: fixed pitch propellers and variable pitch propellers. Fixed pitch propellers have blades set at a constant angle, making them simpler and cheaper, suitable for small aircraft or specific conditions. In contrast, variable pitch propellers allow for adjustable blade angles, optimizing performance across various speeds and altitudes, enhancing fuel efficiency and power. This flexibility makes variable pitch propellers popular in commercial and performance-oriented aircraft, catering to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
