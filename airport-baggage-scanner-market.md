<p><strong>The Airport Baggage Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray Scanner</li><li>Millimeter Wave Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airport baggage scanner market comprises primarily two types: X-ray scanners and millimeter wave scanners. X-ray scanners utilize ionizing radiation to create detailed images of baggage contents, allowing security personnel to identify potential threats effectively. Conversely, millimeter wave scanners employ non-ionizing radio waves to detect hidden objects by analyzing the reflections they produce. Both technologies play crucial roles in airport security, enhancing passenger safety and streamlining the baggage screening process while meeting regulatory compliance requirements.</p></p>
