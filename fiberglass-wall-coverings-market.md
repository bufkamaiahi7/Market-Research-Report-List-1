<p><strong>The Fiberglass Wall Coverings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Woven Fabric</li><li>Woven Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiberglass wall coverings market is primarily categorized into two types: non-woven fabric and woven fabric. Non-woven fiberglass wall coverings are made from randomly oriented fibers, offering high durability and ease of installation, making them suitable for various interior designs. Woven fiberglass, on the other hand, consists of interlaced fibers, providing a more textured finish and enhanced strength, ideal for high-traffic areas. Both types are valued for their fire resistance, biological stability, and ability to contribute to a sustainable environment.</p></p>
