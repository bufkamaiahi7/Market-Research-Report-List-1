<p><p>IoT platforms can be categorized into two main types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises platforms are installed locally on a company's own servers, offering enhanced control, security, and compliance, making them suitable for industries with strict data regulations. Cloud-based platforms, on the other hand, leverage remote server infrastructure to provide scalability, flexibility, and ease of access from anywhere. This model is ideal for businesses looking for cost-effective solutions and rapid deployment without the need for extensive hardware investments.</p></p>
