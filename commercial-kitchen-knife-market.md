<p><p>The commercial kitchen knife market is primarily categorized into stamped blade products and forged blade products. Stamped blades are made from a single sheet of metal, offering a lighter weight and affordability, ideal for casual or budget-conscious chefs. In contrast, forged blades are created through a forging process, resulting in a robust, durable knife with superior edge retention and balance, appealing to professionals seeking precision and longevity. Both types cater to different user needs and preferences in culinary environments.</p></p>
