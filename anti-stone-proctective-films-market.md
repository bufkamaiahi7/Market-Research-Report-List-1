<p><p>The Anti-Stone Protective Films market primarily features two types: Shrink Film and Stretch Film. Shrink Film is designed to tightly wrap around surfaces when heated, providing a durable protective layer against stones and debris, often used in automotive and construction applications. In contrast, Stretch Film is elastic and clings to surfaces without heat, offering versatility in securing and protecting various items, making it popular for packaging and logistics. Both types serve to safeguard products from damage during transport and storage.</p></p>
