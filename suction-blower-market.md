<p><p>The suction blower market primarily consists of two types: spiral fans and centrifugal fans. Spiral fans create airflow through a helical design, enabling efficient air movement and low noise levels, ideal for applications requiring gentle handling of materials. In contrast, centrifugal fans utilize a radial airflow design, generating high pressure for robust performance in demanding environments. Both types are essential in industrial applications, ensuring effective ventilation, dust control, and material handling across various sectors, including manufacturing and HVAC systems.</p></p>
