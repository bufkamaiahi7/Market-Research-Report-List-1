<p><p>Microscale 3D printing equipment encompasses various techniques for creating intricate structures at the microscale. Two-Photon Polymerization (TPP) utilizes laser-induced two-photon absorption to achieve high-resolution features, ideal for applications in microelectronics and biomedicine. Single-Photon Polymerization employs standard light sources for polymerization, offering a more straightforward approach but with slightly less resolution. The "Others" category includes emerging technologies and methods that enhance microscale fabrication capabilities, expanding the scope of applications in research and industry.</p></p>
