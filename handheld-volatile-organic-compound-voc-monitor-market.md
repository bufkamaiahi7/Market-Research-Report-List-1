<p><strong>The Handheld Volatile Organic Compound (VOC) Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld VOC monitor market primarily comprises two types: Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID devices use ultraviolet light to ionize VOC molecules, providing rapid and precise measurements across a wide range of compounds. In contrast, MOS sensors operate by detecting changes in resistance as volatile substances interact with a heated sensor, offering a cost-effective solution for monitoring VOC levels. Both types are essential for environmental compliance, workplace safety, and air quality monitoring.</p></p>
