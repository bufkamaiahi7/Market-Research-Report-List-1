<p><p>Healthcare supply chain software can be categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions are installed locally on a healthcare organization's servers, offering control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, providing scalability, lower initial costs, and easier updates. This distinction allows healthcare organizations to choose a solution that best fits their operational needs and resources.</p></p>
