The market is segmented into SAS (Serial Attached SCSI) and SATA (Serial ATA). SAS is preferred for high-performance applications, providing faster data transfer rates, improved scalability, and reliability, making it ideal for enterprise environments. Conversely, SATA offers a cost-effective solution for bulk storage needs, emphasizing capacity over speed, suitable for less demanding workloads. Both serve distinct roles in the hybrid storage ecosystem.</p></p>
