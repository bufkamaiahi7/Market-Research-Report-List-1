<p><p>The dental vacuum pumps market is primarily categorized into two types: wet vacuum and dry vacuum systems. Wet vacuum pumps use liquid, often water, as a means to create suction, making them effective for continuous operation and effective in capturing larger particles. Dry vacuum systems, on the other hand, rely on air displacement without liquids, offering a cleaner and maintenance-friendly option. Each type presents distinct advantages, catering to varied dental practice needs based on efficiency and operational preferences.</p></p>
