<p><p>The 300mm epitaxial wafer market is categorized primarily into P-type and N-type wafers. P-type wafers are doped with acceptor impurities, allowing for the creation of holes for charge carriers, making them suitable for certain electronic and optoelectronic applications. N-type wafers, on the other hand, are doped with donor impurities, facilitating electron flow. Each type serves distinct roles in semiconductor devices, influencing performance, efficiency, and application in various industries such as photovoltaics, electronics, and optoelectronics.</p></p>
