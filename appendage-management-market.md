<p><strong>The Appendage Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Epicardial LAA Closure Devices</li><li>Endocardial LAA Closure Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The appendage management market is divided primarily into epicardial and endocardial left atrial appendage (LAA) closure devices. Epicardial devices are designed to be placed on the outer surface of the heart, offering a minimally invasive option to occlude the LAA and reduce stroke risk. Conversely, endocardial devices are delivered through the heart's interior, targeting the LAA from within, providing an alternative approach for closure. Both types aim to minimize the risk of thrombus formation in patients with atrial fibrillation.</p></p>
