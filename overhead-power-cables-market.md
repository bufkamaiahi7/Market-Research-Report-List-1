<p><strong>The Overhead Power Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Overhead power cables are categorized into three main types based on voltage levels: </p><p>1. **High Voltage (HV)** cables, operating above 35 kV, are used for long-distance transmission, minimizing energy loss.</p><p>2. **Medium Voltage (MV)** cables, ranging from 1 kV to 35 kV, are typically employed for distribution networks within urban or industrial areas. </p><p>3. **Low Voltage (LV)** cables, below 1 kV, are used for local distribution to residential and commercial consumers, ensuring safe and efficient electricity supply. These classifications facilitate appropriate infrastructure planning and system reliability.</p></p>
