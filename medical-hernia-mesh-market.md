<p><ul><li>Absorbable</li><li>Non-Absorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical hernia mesh market is categorized into absorbable and non-absorbable types. Absorbable meshes are designed to dissolve over time, providing temporary support as tissues heal, reducing long-term complications. Non-absorbable meshes, on the other hand, are made from synthetic materials that remain in the body indefinitely, offering permanent reinforcement to the weakened tissue. Both types are used based on specific patient needs, hernia types, and surgical approaches, impacting recovery outcomes and postoperative complications.</p></p>
