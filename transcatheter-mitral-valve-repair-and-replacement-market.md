<p><p>The Transcatheter Mitral Valve Repair and Replacement market encompasses two primary types: repair and replacement. Transcatheter Mitral Valve Repair focuses on techniques such as edge-to-edge repair, which improves valve function by stitching together leaflets. In contrast, Transcatheter Mitral Valve Replacement involves implanting a new valve in cases where repair is insufficient. Both approaches are minimally invasive, offering patients reduced recovery times and lower risk compared to traditional open-heart surgery, making them increasingly popular in cardiac treatments.</p></p>
