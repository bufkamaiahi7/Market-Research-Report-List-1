<p><p>The PVC paste market, produced via the emulsion method, is categorized into homopolymeric and co-polymeric types. Homopolymeric PVC paste is derived from a single type of vinyl chloride monomer, offering good clarity and mechanical properties suitable for various applications. In contrast, co-polymeric PVC paste, created from two or more monomers, enhances flexibility and impact resistance, making it ideal for specialized uses. Both types are utilized in coatings, adhesives, and flexible products, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
