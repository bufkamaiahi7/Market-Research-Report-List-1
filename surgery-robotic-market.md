<p><ul><li>Multi-port Robotic System</li><li>Single-port Robotic System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgery robotic market comprises two main types: multi-port robotic systems and single-port robotic systems. Multi-port systems utilize multiple entry points for instruments, offering enhanced maneuverability and a wide range of surgical applications, which is beneficial for complex procedures. In contrast, single-port systems streamline surgery through a single incision, minimizing scarring and recovery time. Both systems leverage advanced robotics to improve precision, control, and outcomes in minimally invasive surgeries, catering to diverse medical needs.</p></p>
