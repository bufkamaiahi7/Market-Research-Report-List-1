<p><p>The Ultra High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (UHPLC) detectors market includes various types: </p><p>1. **UV-Visible Detectors** measure absorbance at specific wavelengths, suitable for many analytes.</p><p>2. **Refractive Index Detectors** detect changes in refractive index, ideal for non-UV-absorbing compounds.</p><p>3. **Evaporative Light Scattering Detectors (ELSD)** provide sensitivity for non-volatile analytes by detecting light scattering from aerosolized samples.</p><p>4. **Fluorescence Detectors** utilize fluorescence for enhanced sensitivity with specific compounds.</p><p>5. **Other detectors** encompass emerging technologies and specialized applications, expanding analytical capabilities.</p></p>
